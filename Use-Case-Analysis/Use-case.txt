3. Brief Description of Each Use Case

Register Account: New Customer creates an account with details (name, address, phone, email).
Browse/Search Menu: Customer views restaurant menus, searches dishes by name/category, and adds items to cart.
Place Order: Customer selects items, provides delivery address, and confirms the order.
Make Payment: Customer pays via card, cash on delivery, or digital wallet.
Track Order: Customer or Rider views real-time order status (preparing, out for delivery, delivered).
Update Order Status: Restaurant marks order as "Preparing" or "Ready"; Rider updates to "Picked Up" or "Delivered".
Manage Menu (Restaurant): Add/edit/delete dishes and prices.
View Reports (Admin): Generate sales reports, user statistics, etc.
