Lab 3: UML Class Diagram Design (Student Management System)

Identified Classes

Student
Course
Instructor
Enrollment
Department (optional but useful)

Attributes and Relationships

Student: studentID, name, email, dateOfBirth, major
Course: courseID, courseName, credits, semester
Instructor: instructorID, name, email, department
Enrollment: enrollmentID, student (reference), course (reference), grade, enrollmentDate

Relationships:

Student enrolls in many Courses (many-to-many via Enrollment class).
Course is taught by one Instructor (many-to-one).
Instructor belongs to one Department.
Enrollment associates Student and Course.
